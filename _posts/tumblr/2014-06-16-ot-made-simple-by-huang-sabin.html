Genesis
KEY WORDS
Beginning (of) Blessings
KEY THEME
Blessings
KEY PHRASE
‘…and in thee shall all families of the earth be blessed.’ (6 times)
KEY VERSES
“Get thee out… unto a land that I will show thee: and I will make thee a great nation and I will bless thee, and make thy name great; and thou shalt be a blessing: and … in thee shall all families of the earth be blessed.” (Genesis 12:1-3)
KEY LESSON
God created us for blessings;
God chose us to be a blessing to the world.

Exodus
KEY WORDS
Exit (to) Exalt
KEY THEME
Worship
KEY PHRASE
'Let my people go … that they may worship Me.’ (7 times)
KEY VERSES
“Let My son go, that he may serve Me.” (Exodus 4:23)
KEY LESSON
God redeemed you to worship Him.

Leviticus
KEY WORDS
Priests (and) Feasts
KEY THEME
Holiness
KEY PHRASE
'Be holy … for I, the Lord, am holy.’ (5 times)
KEY VERSES
“For I am the Lord your God: ye shall therefore sanctify yourselves, and ye shall be holy; for I am holy … For I am the Lord that bringeth you up out of the land of Egypt, to be your God: ye shall therefore be holy, for I am holy.” (Leviticus 11:44-45)
KEY LESSON
Holiness is command for all; not an option for some.

Numbers
KEY WORDS
Journey (to) Jordan
KEY THEME
God’s Will
KEY PHRASE
'the Commandment of the Lord’ (20 times)
KEY VERSES
“God is not a man that He should lie; neither the son of man that He should repent: hath He said and shall He not make it good? Behold, I have received commandment to bless: and  He hath blessed; and I cannot reverse it.” (Numbers 23:19-20)
KEY LESSON
The statement that 'Obedience brings blessings and disobedience bring dismay’ is not probability; it is a promise.

Deuteronomy
KEY WORDS
Review (then) Renew
KEY THEME
Loving God
KEY PHRASE
'with all your heart and all your soul’ (9 times)
KEY VERSES
“Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God is one LORD: And thou shalt love the LORD thy God with all thine heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy might.” (Deutronomony 6:4-5)
KEY LESSON
True obedience is based on love not legalism.

Joshua
KEY WORDS
Battles (then) Settles
KEY THEME
Lordship
KEY PHRASE
'All the law’ (12 times)
KEY VERSES
“This book of law shall not depart out of thy mouth; but thou shalt meditate there in day and night, that thou mayest observe to do according to all that is written therein: for then thou shalt make thy way prosperous, and then thou shalt have good success” (Joshua 1:8)
KEY LESSON
Partial obedience is disobedience to God.

Judges
KEY WORDS
Cycles (of) SIN
KEY THEME
Sin
KEY PHRASE
'And the children of Israel (again) did evil in the sight of the LORD.’ (8 times)
KEY VERSES
“In those days there was no king in Israel: every man did that which was right is his own eyes.” (Judges 21:25)
KEY LESSON
We need a good definition of sin, a few detectors of sin, and a great Deliverer from sin.

Ruth
KEY WORDS
Return (for) Romance
KEY THEME
Blessing
KEY PHRASE
'The LORD bless’ (4 times)
KEY VERSES
“And Naomi said unto her daughter-in-law, 'Blessed be he of the LORD, who hath not left of his kindness to the living and the dead” (Ruth 2:20)
KEY LESSON
You don’t have to be rich to be a blessing.

1 Samuel
KEY WORDS
Samuel (and) Saul
KEY THEME
Pressure
KEY PHRASE
'The Lord’s annointed’ (14 times)
KEY VERSES
“Wherefore the Lord God of Israel saith …  for them that honour Me  I will honour, and they that despise Me shall be lightly esteemed.” (1 Samuel 2:30)
KEY LESSON
The 'secret’ power for overcoming pressure comes from honouring God.

2 Samuel
KEY WORDS
David (starts) Decline
KEY THEME
Kingship
KEY PHRASE
'God’s chosen/anointed one’ (12 times)
KEY VERSES
“With the merciful Thou wilt show Thyself merciful, and with the upright man Thou wilt show Thyself upright. With the pure Thou wilt show Thyself pure; and with the froward Thou wilt show thyself unsavoury. And the afflicted people Thou wilt save: but Thine eyes are upon the haughty, that Thou mayest bring them down.” (2 Samuel 22:26-28)
KEY LESSON
Only if you put God on the throne of your life and let God remain there will you receive a crown from Him.

1 Kings
KEY WORDS
Solomon (causes) Split
KEY THEME
David’s Throne
KEY PHRASE
'A man on the throne of Israel’ (3 times)
KEY VERSES
“And Elijah came unto the people and said, 'How long halt ye between two opinions? If the Lord be God, follow Him; but if Baal, then follow him'  And the people answered him not a word.” (1 Kings 18:21)
KEY LESSON
Is your life ruled by the King or by a Committee?

2 Kings
KEY WORDS
Kingdoms (into) Captivities
KEY THEME
Idolatry
KEY PHRASE
'evil in the sight of the LORD’ (21 times)
'right in the sight of the LORD’ (8 times)
KEY VERSES
“So these nations feared the LORD, and served their graven images, both their children and their children’s children: as did their fathers, so do they unto this day” (2 Kings 17:41)
KEY LESSON
Don’t rearrange your idols! Remove them!

1 Chronicles
KEY WORDS
Descendants (until) David
KEY THEME
David’s House
KEY PHRASE
'build a house for My name’ (14 times)
KEY VERSES
“Let it be established, that Thy name may be magnified for ever, saying, The LORD of hosts is the God of Israel, even a God to Israel: and let the house of Thy servant be established before Thee” (1 Chronicles 17:24)
KEY LESSON
Let us work to establish God’s house and pray that God will work to establish our home.

2 Chronicles
KEY WORDS
Solomon (and) 'Successors’
KEY THEME
God’s Temple
KEY PHRASE
'humble yourself’ (12 times)
KEY VERSES
“If My people, which are called by My name, shall humble themselves, and pray, and seek My face, and turn from their wicked ways; then I will hear from heaven, and will forgive their sin, and will heal their land.” (2 Chronicles 7:14)
KEY LESSON
Success is never final. Failure is never fatal. It's  humility that counts.

Ezra
KEY WORDS
Temple (and) People
KEY THEME
God’s House
KEY PHRASE
'House of God’ (33 times)
KEY VERSES
“And now for a little space, grace hath been showed from the LORD our God, to leave us remnant to escape, and to give us a nail in His holy place, that our God my lighten our eyes, and give us a little reviving in our bondage.” (Ezra 9:8)
KEY LESSON
Repentance involves both a resolution and a rehabilitation.

Nehemiah
KEY WORDS
Wall (and) Law
KEY THEME
God’s Work
KEY PHRASE
'Remember me , O God’ (4 times)
KEY VERSES
“Remember me, O God, concerning this, and wipe not out my good deeds that I have done for the House of my God, and for the offices thereof” (Nehemiah 13:14)
KEY LESSON
To keep on doing God’s work, ask God, not men to remember what you have done.

Esther
KEY WORDS
Persia (and) Purim
KEY THEME
God’s People
KEY PHRASE
'the Jews’ (51 times)
KEY VERSES
“For if thou holdest thy peace at this time, then there shall enlargement and deliverance arise to the Jews from another place; but thou and thy father’s house shall be destroyed: and who knoweth whether thou art come to the kingdom for such a time as this.” (Esther 4:14)
KEY LESSON
The window of opportunity for you to help God’s people will not stay open forever.

Job
KEY WORDS
Suffering (under) Sovereignty
KEY THEME
Suffering
KEY PHRASE
'The Almighty’ or 'Shaddai’ (31 times)
KEY VERSES
“Though He slay me, yet will I trust Him. Even so, I will defend my own ways before Him.” (Job 13:15)
KEY LESSON
The proper response to suffering is always to worship God.

Psalms
KEY WORDS
Prayer (with) Praise
KEY THEME
Praise
KEY PHRASE
'Praise the Lord’ (31 times)
KEY VERSES
“Praise ye the LORD. I will praise the LORD with my whole heart, in the assembly of the upright, and in congregation.” (Psalm 111:1)
KEY LESSON
A godly man praises God, not only in peace and prosperity, but also in persecution and poverty.

Proverbs
KEY WORDS
Ways (of) Wisdom
KEY THEME
Wisdom
KEY PHRASE
'fear of the Lord’ (17 times)
KEY VERSES
“The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom: and the knowledge of the holy is understanding.” (Proverbs 9:10)
KEY LESSON
The secret of not falling into life’s traps is to be much more concerned about what God thinks of you, than what man thinks of you.

Ecclesiates
KEY WORDS
Labour (of) Vapour
KEY THEME
Life
KEY PHRASE
'Vanity of vanities, all is vanity’ (8 times)
KEY VERSES
“Fear God, and keep His commandments: for this is the whole duty of man.” (Ecclesiastes 12:13)
KEY LESSON
The FUTILITY of life must lead us to FEAR God; the FEAR of God allows us the FREEDOM to enjoy life.

Song of Songs
KEY WORDS
Solomon (and) Shulammite
KEY THEME
Love
KEY PHRASE
'my/thy beloved’ (28 times)
KEY VERSES
“Many waters cannot quench love, neither can the floods drown it: if a man would give all the substance of his house for love, it would utterly be contemned” (Song of Songs 8:7)
KEY LESSON
Love, like a flower, must blossom in its own time.

Isaiah
KEY WORDS
Stump (before) Shoot
KEY THEME
Salvation
KEY PHRASE
'trust, salvation’ (4 times)
'righteousness, salvation’ (10 times)
KEY VERSES
“My righteousness is near; my salvation is gone forth, and my arms shall judge the people; the isles shall wait upon me, and on my arm shall they trust.” (Isaiah 51:5)
KEY LESSON
Trust in God’s righteousness alone for your eternal salvation.

Jeremiah
KEY WORDS
POTS (and) DESPOTS
KEY THEME
Destruction (divine discipline)
KEY PHRASE
'sword and famine’ (28 times)
KEY VERSES
“See, I have this day set thee over the nations and over the kingdoms, to root out, and to pull down, and to destroy, and to throw down, to build, and to plant.” (Jeremiah 1:10)
KEY LESSON
If we refuse to repent, then God has to break us, in order that He may remake us.

Lamentations
KEY WORDS
Mourning (before) Morning
KEY THEME
Mourning
KEY PHRASE
'punishment of sin/inquity’ (4 times)
KEY VERSES
“It is good that a man should both hope and quietly wait for the salvation of the LORD” (Lamentations 3:26)
KEY LESSON
When we are punished by God, our only hope lies in turning to our 'Enemy’.

Ezekiel
KEY WORDS
Babylon (and) Bones
KEY THEME
Knowledge of God
KEY PHRASE
'Then they will know that I am the LORD’ (65 times)
KEY VERSES
“So will I make my holy name known in the midst of my people Israel; and I will not let them pollute my holy name anymore: and the heathen shall know that I am the LORD, the Holy One in Israel” (Ezekiel 39:7)
KEY LESSON
Stop polluting God’s holiness now, or learn of His holiness through judgement later.

Daniel
KEY WORDS
Den (before) End
KEY THEME
Understanding
KEY PHRASE
’(God gave ) wisdom’ (6 times)
'understand (the vision)’ (11 times)
KEY VERSES
“Many shall be purified, and made white, and tried; but the wicked shall do wickedly: and none of the wicked shall understand; but the wise shall understand.” (Daniel 12:10)
KEY LESSON
If you will do what is right, no matter what the cost; then God will always tell you what to do, no matter what the crisis.

Hosea
KEY WORDS
Harlot (without) Hesed
KEY THEME
Lovingkindness
KEY PHRASE
'did not know God’ (7 times)
KEY VERSES
“Who is wise, and he shall understand these things? prudent, and shall know them? for the ways of the LORD are right, and the just shall walk in them: but the transgressors shall fall therein.” (Hosea 14:9)
KEY LESSON
Know that God’s love must be though.

Joel
KEY WORDS
Plague (and) Pentecost
KEY THEME
Judgement
KEY PHRASE
'the day of the LORD’ (5 times)
KEY VERSES
“The sun shall be turned into darkness, and the moon into blood, before the great and terrible day of the LORD come. And it shall come to pass, that whosoever shall call upon” (Joel 2:31-32)
KEY LESSON
Do you need God to strip you of everything before you repent?

Amos
KEY WORDS
Plumbline (and) Poor
KEY THEME
Justice
KEY PHRASE
'justice and righteousness’ (3 times)
KEY VERSES
“But let judgement (i.e. justice) run down as waters, and righteousness as a mighty stream.” (Amos 5:24)
KEY LESSON
Our righteousness is not measured by our religious ritual; it is measured by our responsibility to deal justly with oppressed.

Obadiah
KEY WORDS
Edom (suffering) Doom
KEY THEME
Judgement
KEY PHRASE
'in the day’ (8 times)
KEY VERSES
“For the day of the LORD is near upon all the heathen: as thou hast done, it shall be done unto thee: thy reward shall return upon thine own head.” (Obadiah 1:15)
KEY LESSON
God 'rewards’ you in the way that you 'reward’ others.

Jonah
KEY WORDS
Fish (and) Fast
KEY THEME
Compassion
KEY PHRASE
’repentest of the evil’ (2 times)
KEY VERSES
“Thou art a gracious God, and merciful, slow to anger, and of great kindness, and repentest Thee of evil.” (Jonah 4:2)
KEY LESSON
Demonstrate God’s compassion to a desperate, dying world.

Micah
KEY WORDS
Cities (of) Shepherd
KEY THEME
True religion
KEY PHRASE
'the remnant’ (6 times)
KEY VERSES
“He hath showed thee, O man, what is good; and what doth the LORD require of thee, but to do justly, and to love mercy, and walk humbly with thy God.” (Micah 6:8)
KEY LESSON
True religion is characterised, not by wealth, but by justice, mercy,  and humility in the face of God’s judgement.

Nahum
KEY WORDS
Flood (and) Fire
KEY THEME
Retribution
KEY PHRASE
'no more’ (4 times)
KEY VERSES
“The Lord is slow to anger, and great in power, and will not at all acquit the wicked.” (Nahum 1:3)
KEY LESSON
Do not mistake the patience of God as the impotence  of God.

Habakkuk
KEY WORDS
Faith (in) Famine
KEY THEME
Faith
KEY PHRASE
'for the violence’ (5 times)
KEY VERSES
“Behold, his soul which is lifted up is not up-right in him: but the just shall live by his faith.” (Habakkuk 2:4)
KEY LESSON
Are you trusting God only when it’s bright? Or also when it’s night?

Zephaniah
KEY WORDS
D-Day (before)  V-Day
KEY THEME
God’s Wrath
KEY PHRASE
’befoeThe day of the LORD’ (7 times)
KEY VERSES
'before the day of the LORD’s anger come upon you, seek ye the LORD, all ye meek of the earth, which hath wrought His judgement; seek rightheousness, seek meekness: it may be ye shall be hid in the day of the Lord’s anger. (Zephaniah 2:2-3)
KEY LESSON
If you don’t want to  perish like the pagans, then don't  live like the pagans.


Haggai
KEY WORDS
House (of) Holiness
KEY THEME
Temple
KEY PHRASE
'Give careful thought’ (5 times in the NIV)
KEY VERSES
“Ye looked for much, and, lo, it came to little; and when ye brought it home,  I did blow upon it. Why?  saith the LORD of hosts. Because of mine house is waste, and ye run every man unto his own house.” (Haggai 1:9)
KEY LESSON
Remember to put doing God’s work first and last.

Zechariah
KEY WORDS
Priest (and) Prince
KEY THEME
Restoration
KEY PHRASE
'in that day’ (20 times)
KEY VERSES
“Thus saith the LORD of hosts; Turn ye unto me, saith the LORD of hosts, and I will turn unto you, saith the LORD of  hosts.” (Zechariah 1:3)
KEY LESSON
Because God is always ready to restore, we must always be ready to repent.

Malachi
KEY WORDS
Messenger (of) Messiah
KEY THEME
God’s Name
KEY PHRASE
'my name’ (7 times)
KEY VERSES
“Then they that feared the LORD spake often to one another: and the LORD hearkened, and heard it, and a book of rememberance was written before him for them that feared the LORD, and that thought upon his name.” (Malachi 3:16)
KEY LESSON
Cursed are all those who dishonour God by their superficial religion.
