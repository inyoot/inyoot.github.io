Don’t be afraid, but neither underestimate the difficulty. A simplified image of the task leads to underestimating the difficulty. Overestimate how hard it will be, take it on right away, and after you reach the peak it will steadily get easier.

Make a flowchart describing operation of the program as a first step.

To get going, do work for several hours, as opposed to meta-work.
