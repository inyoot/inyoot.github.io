One man boasted, “My bow is so good, it needs no arrow.”

Another man boasted, “My arrow so good, it needs no bow.”

The skilled archer Yi heard them and said, “Without a bow, how can you shoot an arrow? And without an arrow how can you hit the target?”

So the bow and the arrow were used together, and Yi taught these men to shoot.

(Han Fei Zi)
