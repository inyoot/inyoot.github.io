A blind man and his dog walk down the road. The dog begins to piss on his legs. 

The blind man offers the dog a cookie. 

A man next him to says “ Why are you giving him a cookie for when your dog is pissing on your legs?”. 

The blind man replied,“So when my dog stands up to reach for the cookie, I can kick him in the ass.”

(One Minute Manager Series by Kenneth Blanchard)
