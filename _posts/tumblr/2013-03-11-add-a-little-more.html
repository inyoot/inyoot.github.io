We recall the kind old grocer,
when the sugar he would pour;
How he carefully tilted the balance;
then he would add a little more.

And his business - how is prospered,
folks would flock into his store;
For he gave an honest measure;
then he would add a little more.

And so it is with life my students,
you can make a bigger score;
If when you do what is expected;
you just added a little more.
