A man walking along the river bank saw someone about to throw a small boy into the water. The child screaming with terror.

“Why do you want to throw that child into the river? asked the passer-by.

"His father is a good swimmer,” was the answer.

But it does not follow that the son of good swimmer can swim.

(The Discourses of Lu Buwei)
